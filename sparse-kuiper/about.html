We Built This Because Nobody Else Would

Workplace Wellness Africa exists to close the gap between what employees need and what workplaces provide — across the continent.

What We Are

Workplace Wellness Africa is a consulting and advocacy organisation. We train businesses to create the structural conditions for work-life balance — financial literacy, mental wellbeing education, policy design — and we use the revenue from that work to fund something most organisations will not touch.

"In too many workplaces, an employee who cannot sleep is given a pill. An employee who is depressed is told they are under spiritual attack. We exist to change what happens next."

Our advocacy targets three systemic failures: the misattribution of mental illness to witchcraft or demonic causes, the over-prescription of sleeping medication as a substitute for proper mental health referral, and workplace leave policies so rigid that employees cannot access therapy without a physical sick note from a clinical doctor.

How This Works

Consulting Revenue

Organisations pay us to deliver training and audits on financial literacy, mental wellbeing, and workplace policy. This is how the mission sustains itself.

Advocacy & Reform

Revenue funds our charitable work: intervening in cases of mental health misattribution, pushing for evidence-based treatment protocols, and campaigning for humane leave policies.
