How We Help

Workplace Wellness Africa connects institutions with the right partners to build sustainable mental health and financial support systems — through education, advocacy, and practitioner networks.

We are a coordinating body that brings together corporations, banks, insurance companies, schools, hospitals, county governments, and community organisations. We don't deliver services alone — we connect the right people, raise awareness among leaders, and advocate for systemic change.
