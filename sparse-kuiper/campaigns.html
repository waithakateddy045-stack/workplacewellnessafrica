Advocacy in Action

We advocate for a professional environment where mental health is a protected right.

Active Advocacy

The Right to Disconnect

In the digital age, the boundary between professional duty and private restoration has vanished. Our "Always-On" culture is driving a silent epidemic of burnout.

Our Objective: Advocating for a cultural shift where employees are not expected to answer work calls or emails outside of agreed working hours.

Our Approach

Encouraging employers to adopt "Quiet Hours" and respect personal time as essential for long-term productivity.

Policy Awareness

Psychosocial Safety

Mental health hazards—like bullying and unmanageable workloads—are just as real as physical hazards.

Our Objective: To raise awareness that psychosocial risks should be taken as seriously as physical safety risks in the workplace.

Our Approach

Educating leaders on ISO 45003 guidelines and how to identify stress hazards before they cause harm.

Awareness Campaign

Financial Integrity & Wellness

Delayed salary is a major cause of mental distress. Financial certainty is a key component of employee wellbeing.

Our Objective: Advocating for timely payment as a fundamental wellness metric and basic professional right.

Our Approach

Providing financial literacy resources and advocating for ethical employment practices.
