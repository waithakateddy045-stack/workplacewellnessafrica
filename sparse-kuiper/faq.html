About Our Services

What exactly does Workplace Wellness Africa do?

We work with organisations across Kenya and East Africa to build healthier, more productive workplaces. Our services include corporate wellness audits, employee mental health training, leadership workshops, and policy advisory. We also provide educational resources through our free article library — covering burnout, work-life balance, toxic leadership, financial stress, and more.

Revenue from our consulting services directly funds our charitable work: fighting mental health stigma and advocating for humane workplace policies.

Who are your services designed for?

We serve two audiences:

  • Organisations: HR teams, business leaders, and corporate decision-makers who want to improve employee wellbeing, reduce turnover, and build healthier workplace cultures.
  • Individuals: Working professionals who want to better understand burnout, set boundaries, navigate toxic environments, or simply improve their mental health at work.
What industries do you work with?

We work across all sectors — including manufacturing, financial services, NGOs, education, healthcare, technology, and government. Workplace stress doesn't discriminate by industry, and neither do we. Our programmes are always customised to fit the unique challenges of each organisation.

Do you offer services outside Kenya?

Yes. While we are headquartered in Kenya, we serve organisations across East Africa and are expanding to other African markets. Our workshops and training can be delivered in-person or virtually, making it easy to work with teams in Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, and beyond.

How It Works

How do I get started with your corporate services?

It's simple:

  1. Reach out: Fill in our contact form or message us on WhatsApp.
  2. Discovery call: We schedule a free 30-minute call to understand your organisation's needs.
  3. Custom proposal: We design a tailored programme — whether it's a one-off workshop, a full wellness audit, or an ongoing partnership.
  4. Delivery: We deliver the programme, track outcomes, and provide a report with actionable recommendations.
How long does a typical engagement last?

It depends on what you need:

  • Single workshop: Half-day or full-day session
  • Wellness audit: 2–4 weeks (assessment + report)
  • Ongoing partnership: Monthly or quarterly check-ins over 6–12 months

We're flexible and design around your timeline and budget.

Can workshops be delivered virtually?

Absolutely. We've delivered virtual workshops to teams across East Africa using Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet. Virtual sessions are especially effective for remote/hybrid teams and organisations with offices in multiple locations.

Confidentiality & Trust

Is employee data kept confidential?

100%. All employee responses from our audits, surveys, and assessments are anonymised and aggregated. We never share individual responses with employers. The reports we provide to organisations contain only aggregate insights — not individual names or responses.

We take confidentiality seriously because trust is the foundation of effective wellness work. Without it, employees won't be honest, and the data becomes meaningless.

What happens to my personal assessment data?

Our self-assessment tools run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your responses to any server. Your results are yours alone. Nobody else sees them — not us, not your employer, not anyone.
