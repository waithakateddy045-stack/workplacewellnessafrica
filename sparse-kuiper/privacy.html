Who We Are

Workplace Wellness Africa ("we", "our", "us") is a workplace wellness organisation based in Kenya. We provide corporate wellness consulting, employee training, educational resources, and advocacy for mental health in African workplaces.

What Data We Collect

We collect minimal data, and only what is necessary to provide our services:

  • Contact Forms: When you submit a contact or enquiry form, we collect your name, email address, phone number (if provided), and message content.
  • Newsletter Signups: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address.
  • Self-Assessment Tools: Our burnout self-assessment and other interactive tools run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your assessment responses. Your results remain on your device only.
  • Chatbot Interactions: Our website chatbot runs locally in your browser. Conversations are not sent to any server or third party.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to remember your preferences (such as cookie consent). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.
